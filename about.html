Collective Mission

Building the Future of Celestial Public Art

Nebula Collective is a nonprofit studio advancing the intersection of robotics, storytelling, and STEM access through drone shows, education, and open tools.

Purpose

Democratize the Sky

We turn advanced aerial systems into experiences and curriculum that communities can access, learn from, and own.

Method

Open + Applied

Our production stack is tested in public events, then translated into workshops, documentation, and student-ready labs.

Outcome

Local Capability

Partner cities and schools gain repeatable playbooks for safe launches, creative direction, and operations.

Timeline

Key moments from research to regional deployment.

2021

First Open Swarm Curriculum

Piloted a 12-week systems course covering flight control, choreography, and field safety.

2023

Multi-City Public Show Network

Scaled synchronized productions across municipal partners with a reusable launch framework.

2025

Educator Fellowship Program

Launched fellowships to help teachers bring autonomous systems and visual storytelling into classrooms.

Support the Mission
