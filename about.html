About Nebula

Nebula is powered by a small team with overlapping skills in visual art, technical production, education, and community-facing program design.

Executive Director

Brandon

Origin: Michigan

Brandon is dedicated to expanding access to creative technology education. He brings expertise in grant development and serves as Nebula's resident DJ.

Team Member

Bryan

Origin: Texas

Bryan is a recent graduate of the University of Texas Arts and Entertainment Technology program. Bryan brings a refined design eye and expertise in multiple digital and physical media.

Team Member

Kacey

Origin: Colorado

Kacey is one of the world's first female drone show pilots and a veteran drone show designer. In her free time she writes, acts, and spins, breathes, and eats fire. Kacey does not like pants.

Team Member

Mason

Origin: Michigan

Mason is passionate about drones as an art medium and conversation starter. He teaches drone show design at the University of Texas and is the founder of Adhoc Creative House.

Team Member

Shawn

Origin: New Jersey

Shawn is hardware, software, lights, guitarist, mechanic. He can 3d-print a 4-foot tall human heart in 78 different pieces, or build you a timecode app just for fun.

Team Member

Tony

Origin: New Jersey

Tony is an original founder of Verge Aero and an AI / machine learning expert. He bridges the gap between hardware and software and remains on the cutting edge of tech advances.
